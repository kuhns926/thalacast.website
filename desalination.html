Coastal Desalination

The 1 MGD Coastal Anchor

Positioned strategically on the far corner of the coastline to preserve beachfront living, our 1 Million Gallon Per Day (MGD) desalination plant secures the city's water independence.

Zero Liquid Discharge Brine Processing

ThalaCast operates with a commitment to the coastal ecosystem. We utilize advanced brine processing units to extract usable minerals and clean salt from the brine.

Fueling the Night Shift

To run our nighttime sCO2 power turbines, we must produce 11 metric tons of hydrogen daily. The desalination plant easily supplies the 99,000 liters of ultra-pure deionized water required by our 65 MW electrolysis plant.
