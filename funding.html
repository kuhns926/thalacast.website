Current Mission

Our proprietary value is in the symbiotic integration of systems. ThalaCast acts as a real-world blueprint for solving drought, power grid failure, and zero-emission housing. ThalaBlocks are the core building material. They will be properly built, tested, and certified. This is Phase Zero.

The brilliance of having somewhere useful to indefinitely store salt that has been getting released into our lands, destroying our rivers and oceans, is unparalleled. ThalaCast makes drinking water by repurposing the desalination waste. The waste is captured and used to erect buildings. Recycled plastics are the other half of the foundational construction. They will be heavily consumed, pulling thousands and thousands of pounds of plastic out of the waste stream.

Join the revolutionary track of making living affordable to all. Not just at the point of extraction. Not just to new ways of building cities. But for the world. For those in need.
