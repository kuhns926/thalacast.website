The 10 MW AI Compute Block

Integrated Urban Computing & The Daylight Science Engine

We have the capability to create a symbiotic relationship with artificial intelligence. From sun to the sea, all natural resources that are almost infinite, create a civilization that can not only function, but provide for the future of science.

Our servers are no ordinary servers. We only sell our computing power to causes that benefit mankind. During Phase 1 of our city buildout, our 10 MW AI center operates as a Daylight Science Engine. We power it strictly during peak solar hours, offering zero-emission "Green Spot Compute" to universities for climate modeling, biotech, and material sciences. This protects our municipal battery reserves for the city's night operations.

Once our custom Oxy-Hydrogen sCO2 turbines come online in Phase 2, we unlock continuous, day-and-night 24/7 compute. We are not just building solar farms and wasting our lands. We build and integrate straight into the humanitarian cause. As many jobs as we can produce. As many affordable houses that ThalaCast can produce. As many people as possible will benefit from our project.

Whether you like it or not, artificial intelligence is not going anywhere. We might as well create a world where we work and thrive together.

Unprecedented Efficiency

Running our massive cooling lines of the AI servers through a loop of coolant of a massive waterpark gives nearly free cooling to the Brain of the city. It brings us to a Power Usage Effectiveness (PUE) of ~1.02. We provide a perfectly chilled 10 MW AI center and perfectly heated pools without spending a single extra watt of electricity. Not even to mention we have a giant waterpark attraction that brings joy to the community.
