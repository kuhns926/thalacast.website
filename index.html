Welcome

ThalaCast is more than just a city. It lives and breathes. It takes in the sun and sea and exhales rust and water. This is a negative carbon built industrial power plant. We do not waste. We build from waste. We go from waste to gold. We are solving many problems all at once.

We will provide affordable housing. We will provide freshwater and power. We build with the very poison, the brine leftover from desalination, and process it down to pure distilled water.

Our night shift power runs off of Supercritical Carbon-Dioxide, pure hydrogen, and pure oxygen. The exhaust is literally pure water that loops right back through the system pre-heating the next cycle. Our Iron-Air batteries provide a deep load safety net and our Lithium Iron Phosphate provide instant power spikes. At ThalaCast we backup the backup.

The Grid

The importance of the five nines grid. This means that we generate power producing 99.999% run time. This is critical for what we are trying to achieve. A symbiotic relationship with AI. We have created a city where the housing blocks serve a purpose. We build the power grid within the city blocks. We have alleyways between the backs of every house. A solar canopy runs down the block, providing power to the grid and shade to the battery backup that is located in the alleyways. This is where most of our Iron-Air batteries and LFPs (Lithium Iron Phosphate batteries) are located. The city streets, same thing. There is a solar canopy over every city block. Not to mention every parking lot that is loaded with solar canopies.

The most important part of this entire project lies within the business model. We are running a not-for-profit city. Every dime that pores in, stays in house. The original model is that of 1,000 housing units. I am confident we will expand way beyond that, but for now that is the target goal. We provide a downtown area of shops and entertainment, and we sell everything at near cost. We do not need massive profits. We need enough to keep our city running and more than likely expanding beyond.

Desalination

The secret to all of this is the sun and the sea. We process sea water and create freshwater and a toxic brine that currently gets dumped right back into the ocean destroying sea life. This is not sustainable. We need to do something with all of that salt. Our basic building block for our sustainable city is a brick composed of a salt core and a plastic shell. The core will be wrapped in recycled PET plastic. We designed these similar to a lego type block. We call these our iBlocks. Every wall of each house, every pillar of the community, every building we can construct out of them will be built with the iBlock. Our Bones Factory produces more than just blocks. We are using the PET mixtures to extrude our iBeams and iC-Channel. All three of these basic building materials are made from waste brine and the world’s plastic waste. Not only are we a zero-carbon civilization, we build with the very products that are basically considered waste.

ThalaCast is designed literally from the bottom up. We have no corporate interests. We have a fully autonomous city that can afford to pay its workers a good living wage while thriving in their own community. We need only one investment, one time. The city will produce most of what it needs.

AI Data Center

Last but not least, the AI incorporation. We are creating so much excess power just from the city blocks and parking lots that we can afford to spend the excess on AI. Our servers will be liquid cooled with our pure distilled water that we are making in the process of mining our brine solution. We try to waste zero and aim to waste zero. The giant waterpark, a one million gallon heat sink for the cooling of the AI servers, uses the heat of the servers to climate control the pool while getting “free” cooling. Not to mention the revenue that will be generated from the sales of our server usage.
