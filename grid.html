The Five Nines Microgrid

The Decentralized Power Spine

ThalaCast generates its power directly within the city blocks. We have eliminated the need for distant, ecologically destructive solar farms. Instead, a massive PETG iBeam solar canopy runs down every street, parking lot, and alleyway. This architectural integration, combined with the roofs of our industrial sectors, provides continuous shade while creating a decentralized power plant.

Our microgrid boasts a 100+ MW solar capacity, generating upwards of 500 MWh of pure energy per day. Because our housing units are hyper-efficient, this massive surplus allows us to power heavy industrial factories, a desalination plant, and our Daylight Science Engine without pulling a single watt from outside the city.

Phase 1: Municipal Five Nines

To keep the 1,000 residential units pristine and minimalist, our energy storage is physically built into the alleyway canopies. We utilize a two-tier battery architecture to achieve "Municipal Five Nines" uptime from day one.

  • 40 MWh of Lithium Iron Phosphate (LFP): For instantaneous load-smoothing and absorbing rapid power spikes from our industrial presses.
  • 100 MWh of Iron-Air Batteries: For deep, multi-day load resilience.

Even if the sun is blocked by severe weather for days, the city's life support—homes, water, refrigeration, and emergency services—will not falter. We back up the backup.

Phase 2: Industrial Five Nines & The Hydrogen Loop

True "Five Nines" (99.999%) uptime for heavy industry and 24/7 AI computing cannot rely on the weather. That is why ThalaCast is designed as an incubator for the next generation of zero-emission baseload power: Oxy-Hydrogen Supercritical CO2 (sCO2) Turbines.

During peak solar hours, we route our massive 500 MWh excess power to 20 MW electrolyzers. These machines crack our pure, desalinated distilled water into pure Hydrogen and pure Oxygen, which we safely compress and store in specialized on-site tanks.

At night, we inject this pure $H_2$ and $O_2$ into our supercritical $CO_2$ loop. Because we use pure oxygen instead of atmospheric air, there is zero nitrogen present, meaning absolutely zero toxic $NO_x$ emissions are produced. The extreme heat spins our 5 MW custom turbines, and the only exhaust produced by the combustion is pure, superheated steam. We condense that steam back into liquid distilled water, route it back to the electrolyzers, and start the cycle completely over. It is a 100% closed-loop system.
